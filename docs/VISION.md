# Persnally — Vision (locked)

> The fixed reference. The **vision** does not move; **strategy** flexes beneath it
> ([PIVOT.md](../PIVOT.md)). When a competitor ships or a demo wobbles, the route
> changes — not this. Locked 2026-06-17.

## The vision

**Persnally is the intelligence that makes every AI know _you_ — a model of yourself
that is _yours_: you own it, no vendor can hold it, and it gets sharper the more AI you
use. As AI becomes how everyone interfaces with everything, whoever owns their context
owns their leverage. Persnally keeps that with the person.**

## The two bets it rests on

1. **AI personalization is inevitable and universal.** Every AI is more useful the more
   it knows you; the whole industry is already racing on memory/context. (~95%)
2. **Ownership is the durable axis — and structurally only the user can hold it.** A
   neutral, cross-vendor model of you is something no vendor can offer without
   contradicting its own business, so it falls to the user side. (~85%)

These are reads on where the world is going, not on this quarter. They do not get
re-validated every cycle.

## The through-line: _yours_ is the promise, local-first is the proof

- **"Yours" is the promise** — the thing we say and sell: you own your model of yourself.
- **Local-first + source-available + deletable is the proof** — the architecture that
  makes the promise credible.

Privacy is **not the pitch**; it is the _receipt_. We don't lead with "private" — it's
defensive, commoditized, and over-claims the moment cloud inference touches data. We lead
with **yours**, and prove it with a local event log you can audit and a delete that
actually works. You can always promise "it's yours"; you cannot always promise "private."

## Fixed vs flexible

A "pivot" is a change in the **flexible** column. The fixed column is the company.

## Brand lines (synced to the vision)

- **Hero / tagline:** _So every AI finally knows you._ — keep; it is the vision.
- **Descriptor (npm / GitHub):** _Your own context engine — local-first, across every
  AI._ Was "The context engine **for you**" — "for you" no longer differentiates; the
  weight moves to **your own** + the local-first / cross-AI wedge.
- **Pitch:** _The giants build the intelligence. Persnally makes it yours._ — keep;
  "makes it yours" is the through-line.
- **Retired:** "the context engine _for you_" as a positioning line; "private" as a
  headline word; the "they built it for agents, we built it for you" contrast (the
  "only we say _for you_" claim is falsified — others now say it too).

## What the vision is _not_ (guardrails)

- **Not "AI memory / portability."** That's the commodity bus; everyone ships it. We are
  the intelligence and the ownership on top, not the memory layer.
- **Not a privacy vault.** Ownership is active leverage, not passive secrecy.
- **Not dev-infra.** A product for a person who uses AI — not a memory SDK for people
  building agents. The infra seat is contested and hard to hold; the
  person-owned-intelligence seat is open.
